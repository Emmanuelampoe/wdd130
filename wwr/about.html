History

Dry Oar was founded over two decades ago with a simple vision: to open the wild, untamed rivers of nature to adventurers from all walks of life. In our early days, our small but dedicated team built our first raft from reclaimed materials and tested our mettle against turbulent river currents. Over the years, we have grown into a full-service rafting company, proudly upholding our traditions while embracing modern techniques and equipment.
